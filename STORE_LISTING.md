## Full description

Drug Wars Reloaded is a fictional offline trading strategy game inspired by classic calculator-era street sims — rebuilt with original code, neon UI, and deep empire mechanics.

Travel across 15 cities and dozens of districts. Buy and sell product when prices swing. Manage heat, pay down debt to the loan shark, hire crew, fulfill contracts, buy properties, and grow legitimate fronts that launder cash.

**Features**
- Fully offline — no account, no server, no waiting
- Market trading with live price trends per district
- Story missions, daily objectives, and rank progression
- Suppliers, contracts, crew, properties, and businesses
- Choice-based street encounters and world events
- Local save on your device — reset anytime

**Important**
This is a work of fiction. It does not provide real-world instructions for illegal activity. For mature audiences.

**Version 1.0.0 note:** In-app purchases are **not enabled** in this release. The base game is fully playable without purchases.
